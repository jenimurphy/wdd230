COC - TARGET AUDIENCE & SCENARIOS

TARGET AUDIENCE

Port Townsend is a small, yet bustling community that is family friendly. Families can find the information they need here at the Chamber to live and thrive in our community. Within a short drive to Seattle or a ferry ride away from local islands, Port Townsend is a place to come and stay awhile!

The small to medium businesses that support the tourism that is always flowing through the Port Townsend, will find the resources they need to get started. Business can connect with and support each other here at the Chamber of Commerce. This is the place to grow with the community and other local businesses.

SCENARIOS

COMMUNITY INFO - What is it like to live in Port Townsend? What is the average weather? How is the traffic? How are the schools?

RESTAURANTS - What are the sit-down restaurants in town? Are there any big fast food chains?

ACCOMODATIONS - What kind of accomodations are available?

EVENTS - What are the annual events that take place in Port Townsend? Are there business events? What events are sponsored by the Chamber of Commerce?

DIRECTORY - Search for the businesses in town by different categories

Sally has always wanted to have her own Bed & Breakfast. She has planned to start this business when she retired. Sally is looking for the right area that has good tourism as well as support from the local businesses and community.
