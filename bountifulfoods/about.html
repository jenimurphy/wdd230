ABOUT BOUNTIFUL FOODS

Bountiful Foods was first opened in 1987 with a stand at the local farmer‚Äôs market on Wednesdays in the Village. We are a small owner-operated business specializing in organic, locally grown foods in Carlsbad, California.

With our store-front and food trucks, we cater to tourists and our local communities of the coastal area from Los Angeles to San Diego. We sell fruits and produce directly from the farmers in the area and some national and international suppliers. The walk-in traffic at our store-front in Carlsbad Village can order at the counter, or call in an order over the phone, or via the website.

Our mission is to provide fresh and healthy food options, including organic, made-to-order fruit drinks and fruit bags, to the local population and tourists visiting the coastal areas.
