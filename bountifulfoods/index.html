Carlsbad Highlights

Carlsbad, California is situated just 35 miles north of San Diego and has many attractions and beaches close by.

Visit the historic downtown district, Carlsbad Village and go to the Farmer's Market on Wednesdays or plan a few days at Legoland.

Relax with a day or two at one of the most beautiful stretches of coastline at the South Carlsbad Beach. Carlsbad is the perfect family-friendly vacation destination.
