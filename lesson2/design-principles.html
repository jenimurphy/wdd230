DESIGN PRINCIPLES DOCUMENT

Jeniffer Murphy

Alignment

Good Books
goodbooks.com

The Good Books website is left-aligned, starting right from the top with the logo, continuing down the page. Their homepage uses the clean design-white space design principle as well. This alignment and clean design keeps the focus on finding YOUR next favorite book.

Contrast

T-Mobile
t-mobile.com

T-Mobile uses their company's main color with a contrast of white in the background a shade of pink for the text. This pink contrasts nicely with the phone they are featuring on their homepage. This color is used all over their website which brings in the repetitive design principle as well.

White Space

Apple
apple.com

Apple consistently uses clean designs with the plenty of "white" space on their website. Their homepage starts with the black background as their "white space" with the image of their focus being aligned in the center of the page. There is no other distraction. It is clear they are wanting you to focus on that particular phone.
